Commercial HVAC Installation

New commercial HVAC installations require careful planning, precise sizing, and expert installation to ensure optimal performance and energy efficiency. Our commercial installation team works with architects, contractors, and building owners to design and install systems that meet specific operational requirements and budget constraints.

Rooftop unit installation is common for commercial buildings due to space efficiency and easy maintenance access. We install and service all major brands of rooftop units, ensuring proper electrical connections, refrigerant charging, and control system programming for optimal operation.

Split system installations provide flexible zoning options for businesses with varying heating and cooling needs throughout different areas. We design multi-zone systems that provide individual temperature control while maintaining energy efficiency.

VRF and VRV Systems

Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) and Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV) systems provide the highest level of energy efficiency and comfort control for commercial applications. These systems allow simultaneous heating and cooling in different zones while using sophisticated inverter technology to match capacity with demand.

VRF systems are ideal for office buildings, hotels, and multi-tenant facilities where different areas have varying comfort requirements. Our technicians are factory-trained on major VRF brands including Mitsubishi, Daikin, and LG to provide expert installation and service.

Commercial Ductless Mini Split Systems

Ductless mini split systems provide efficient, quiet operation ideal for offices, retail spaces, and other commercial applications where traditional ductwork is impractical. These systems offer individual zone control, easy installation, and superior energy efficiency.

Multi-zone ductless systems can provide heating and cooling for entire commercial buildings while maintaining individual temperature control in each area. This flexibility makes them ideal for businesses with varying occupancy patterns and comfort requirements.

Preventive Maintenance Programs

Commercial HVAC systems require regular maintenance to operate efficiently and reliably. Our preventive maintenance programs are designed to minimize downtime, reduce energy costs, and extend equipment life. Regular maintenance also helps maintain manufacturer warranty coverage.

Scheduled maintenance visits are planned around your business operations to minimize disruption. We work with facility managers to schedule maintenance during off-hours or low-activity periods when possible.

Indoor Air Quality for Businesses

Commercial indoor air quality directly impacts employee productivity, customer comfort, and health. Our commercial air quality solutions include advanced filtration, UV sanitization, and ventilation improvements designed for business environments.

MERV 13-16 filtration removes airborne contaminants including dust, pollen, bacteria, and viruses. High-efficiency filtration is especially important for medical facilities, schools, and businesses where air quality is critical.

UV-C sanitization systems destroy bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms in HVAC systems and air streams. These systems provide an additional layer of protection for businesses concerned about airborne disease transmission.

Restaurant and Kitchen HVAC

Restaurants and commercial kitchens have unique HVAC requirements including grease exhaust, make-up air, and dining area comfort control. Our restaurant HVAC specialists understand these requirements and provide solutions that maintain comfort while meeting health department codes.

Kitchen exhaust systems remove heat, grease, and odors from cooking areas. We install and service commercial exhaust hoods, fans, and ductwork designed for restaurant applications. Proper exhaust ventilation is essential for fire safety and health code compliance.

Make-up air systems replace air exhausted from kitchens while conditioning the incoming air for comfort. These systems must be properly balanced with exhaust systems to maintain building pressure and prevent drafts in dining areas.

Medical and Healthcare HVAC

Medical facilities require specialized HVAC systems that meet strict air quality and ventilation standards. Our healthcare HVAC services include design, installation, and maintenance of systems that comply with medical facility codes and standards.

HEPA filtration and positive pressure systems prevent contamination in surgical suites and clean rooms. These systems require specialized knowledge and equipment to install and maintain properly.

Humidity control is critical in medical facilities to prevent bacterial growth and maintain sterile environments. We install and service commercial humidification and dehumidification systems designed for healthcare applications.

Retail and Office HVAC

Retail stores and office buildings require comfortable environments that encourage productivity and customer satisfaction. Our commercial HVAC solutions provide reliable comfort control while minimizing energy costs and maintenance requirements.

Zone control systems allow different temperature settings for various areas within large buildings. This is especially important for retail spaces with different comfort requirements in sales areas, storage areas, and offices.

Smart thermostat systems automatically adjust temperatures based on occupancy schedules and outdoor conditions. These systems reduce energy consumption during unoccupied periods while ensuring comfortable conditions when employees and customers are present.

Industrial HVAC Solutions

Industrial facilities often have unique heating and cooling requirements due to equipment heat loads, process requirements, and building configurations. Our industrial HVAC solutions address these specific needs while maintaining energy efficiency.

Process cooling systems remove heat generated by industrial equipment and processes. These systems must be designed to handle high heat loads while providing reliable operation to prevent production interruptions.

Ventilation systems remove contaminants, fumes, and excess heat from industrial work areas. Proper ventilation is essential for worker safety and compliance with occupational health standards.

Energy management features automatically optimize system operation based on occupancy, weather conditions, and energy costs. These systems can significantly reduce operating expenses while maintaining comfort for building occupants.
