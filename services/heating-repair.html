Gas Furnace Repair Services

Gas furnaces are popular heating choices in Hueytown due to their reliability and efficiency. Our furnace repair services address all types of problems including ignition system failures, heat exchanger cracks, blower motor issues, and gas valve problems. Safety is our top priority when servicing gas heating equipment.

Ignition system problems are common in modern gas furnaces. Electronic ignition systems have replaced pilot lights in most newer furnaces, requiring specialized diagnostic equipment and procedures. Our technicians are trained on all types of furnace ignition systems and carry replacement components for common failures.

Heat exchanger inspection is a critical safety service we provide. Cracked heat exchangers can allow dangerous carbon monoxide to enter your home's air supply. We use specialized inspection cameras and combustion analysis equipment to detect heat exchanger problems and ensure safe operation.

Heat Pump Repair Expertise

Heat pumps provide both heating and cooling, making them ideal for Alabama's climate. Our heat pump repair services address refrigerant leaks, compressor problems, reversing valve issues, and defrost control malfunctions. We service all major heat pump brands and models.

Reversing valve problems can prevent heat pumps from switching between heating and cooling modes. This component is unique to heat pump systems and requires specialized knowledge for proper diagnosis and repair. Our technicians carry replacement reversing valves for common heat pump models.

Defrost control systems prevent ice buildup on outdoor coils during heating operation. When these systems malfunction, heat pumps can lose efficiency and may stop producing heat altogether. Our diagnostic procedures quickly identify defrost control problems and restore proper operation.

Electric Heating System Repairs

Electric furnaces and heat pumps require different diagnostic and repair approaches than gas systems. Our technicians are skilled in electrical troubleshooting and repair, addressing problems with heating elements, contactors, sequencers, and control systems.

Heating element failures are common in electric furnaces and heat pumps. These components can burn out over time or fail due to electrical problems. We carry replacement heating elements for popular brands and can restore heating capacity quickly and efficiently.

Thermostat and Control System Repairs

Modern heating systems rely on sophisticated control systems for optimal operation. Thermostat problems, control board failures, and sensor malfunctions can prevent proper heating operation. Our diagnostic procedures identify control system problems and restore proper system operation.

Smart thermostat programming and calibration ensure optimal heating performance and energy efficiency. We provide thermostat setup, programming, and calibration services to maximize comfort while minimizing energy consumption throughout the heating season.

Ductwork and Airflow Solutions

Proper airflow is essential for heating system performance and efficiency. Ductwork problems including leaks, restrictions, and poor design can significantly impact heating performance. Our technicians evaluate airflow throughout your system to identify and correct problems.

Duct sealing improves heating efficiency and reduces energy costs. We use professional-grade sealants to repair leaks in accessible ductwork. Properly sealed ductwork ensures heated air reaches living spaces rather than being lost in crawl spaces or attics.

Safety Inspections and Carbon Monoxide Testing

Safety is paramount when servicing heating systems, especially gas-fired equipment. Our comprehensive safety inspections include gas leak testing, carbon monoxide detection, and combustion analysis. We ensure your heating system operates safely and efficiently.

Carbon monoxide testing is performed on all gas heating systems to detect dangerous leaks or improper combustion. We use calibrated CO analyzers to measure carbon monoxide levels and ensure safe operation. Any safety concerns are addressed immediately to protect your family.
